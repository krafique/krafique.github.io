Vision-Based Ice Hockey Agent in PySuperTuxKart

04

Improving Negation Robustness in NLI via Data Augmentation and Oversampling

Diagnosed a systematic negation bias in an ELECTRA-based Natural Language Inference classifier on SNLI, where the model over-predicted contradiction based on surface negation markers. Introduced targeted data augmentation and an oversampling strategy that raised negation-slice accuracy from 80.0% to 84.3% with minimal impact on overall performance.
